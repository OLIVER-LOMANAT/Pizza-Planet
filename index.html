    About Us

    Our Story

    Pizza Planet was founded in 2010 by two best friends who shared a passion for great pizza and space! Our mission is to serve the most delicious pizza in the galaxy while providing a fun experience for the whole family.

     

    All our ingredients are fresh and locally sourced. Our dough is made in-house daily, and sauce is a secret family recipe passed down through generations.

     

    Whether you're dining imn our space-themed restaurant or ordering for delivery, we promise an out-of-this-world pizza experience that will have you coming back for more!
